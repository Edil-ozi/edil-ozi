**Edil Ozi** is a collection of re-usable components that you can copy and paste into your web apps.

Pick the components you need. Copy and paste the source code into your project and customize to your needs. 

Browse through variety of components designed to suit your specific requirements.

## Components
Discover the perfect utility components for your needs, including buttons, inputs, and more... 

## Clean Components
Components built exclusively with [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com/), without any animation libraries.

## Fancy Components
As the name says, fancy components which might enhance your website.

## On Scroll Animations
Animated components that reacts to scrolling.

## Text Animatios
Custom text animations to suit your requirements.
